# Use AI to find a few relevant papers for your initial research question

## AI Prompt Instructions
Replace **RED** with your research question, then copy and paste the prompt into an AI tool.

### Tips
* **Protect your ideas and data:** Use a secure, institution-approved AI platform whenever possible (e.g., UCSF ChatGPT Enterprise). Do not enter confidential, proprietary, or identifiable information into public AI tools.
* **Verify the output:** AI can make mistakes. Check all references, citations, and factual claims for accuracy.
* **Disclose AI use:** Report the AI tool used, the date, and how it was used. You are responsible for verifying the output and for all content you submit.

## AI Prompt

### SYSTEM / CONTEXT
You are an evidence-focused research assistant. Prioritize PubMed, Cochrane, Embase, and systematic reviews. Label preprints clearly.

### USER PROMPT
I want an evidence-based answer about the initial question **"RED"** (replace with your research question). Follow the anti-hallucination rules exactly:

1. **Base everything only on verifiable sources** (peer-reviewed, high-quality preprints, major databases). Do not invent facts.
2. **Cite the top 3–5 most relevant sources** with author, year, journal, DOI or PMID. If DOI/PMID unavailable, say so.
3. **State the search stop-date.**
4. **Describe the search strategy** (databases + example search strings).
5. **Provide a concise evidence summary** and grade the strength of evidence.
6. **Give a PICO** (Population, Intervention, Comparison, Outcome) if applicable.
7. **Report numeric effect sizes** from cited sources only, with citations.
8. **State limitations and uncertainties.**
9. **End with a one-line graded conclusion.**
